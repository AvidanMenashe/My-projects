This is a game in  the Assembly language.
In the game, you are an "O" symbol on the screen.
Your goal is to "eat" the "X" symbol.
Every time you eat an X, your score total will increase by one.


Comments:
1.	If you want to exit, press "q" key.
2.	In order to run the code in your local computer, you need a DOSBOX simulator of the 8086 processor.
